### 🧬 Viral Genomics Pipeline (Nextflow DSL2)

Reproducible viral genomics workflow for Illumina paired-end sequencing data.

- Quality control (FastQC, fastp)
- Read alignment (BWA-MEM)
- BAM processing (Samtools)
- Variant calling (BCFtools)
- Consensus genome generation
- Tested on Ebola virus data: **100% genome coverage, 561 SNPs**

🔗 [GitHub Repository](https://github.com/RojhanGenomics/viral-genomics-pipeline)
